Mutton Biryani

Description

Mutton biryani is the epitome of Indian cuisine, with layers of beautifully spiced mutton

Tender grains of rice topped with coriander and fried onions. Serve with raita for a refreshing combination of heat and flavour.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Mutton
  • green cardamom
  • red chilli
  • garam masala powder
  • turmeric and fresh ghee
  • onions

Steps

  1. Wash the Mutton and then marinate it.
  2. Boil the rice and heat ghee in the pan
  3. Fry the onions
  4. Cook the marinated meat
  5. Layer the rice and mutton
  6. Let the Biryani cook for 15 minutes
