Beef fry

Description

A mouthwatering roadside special from Kerala

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil + 1 teaspoon oil
  • 4-5 shallots, sliced
    • >
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and sliced
  • 1-2 green chilies, sliced
  • ½ teaspoon ground turmeric (turmeric powder)
  • 1/2 - 1 teaspoon red chili powder 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 teaspoon crushed black peppercorns 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a wok or large frying pan or high heat.
  2. Add oil and swirl around to coat the sides of the wok.
    3. >
  3. Add beef. Stir fry 2-3 minutes or until beef is browned and just cooked. Transfer to a plate. Stir fry beef in batches if needed.
  4. Heat 1 teaspoon oil in the wok over medium-high heat. Add mustard seeds and allow to splutter.
  5. Add garlic and ginger. Sauté, stirring, for 1 minute or until fragrant.
  6. Add green chilies (if using). Sauté, stirring, for a few seconds
    7. .
  7. Add ground turmeric, ground coriander and red chili powder. Stir fry for few seconds.
  8. Add beef, garam masala, crushed peppercorns and curry leaves (if using). Stir fry for 1 minute or until the beef strips get a dark brown color.
